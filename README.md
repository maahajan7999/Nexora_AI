# Nexora AI

### Transform Data Into Decisions

Nexora AI is an AI-powered Business Digital Twin Platform that transforms CSV/XLSX datasets into executive dashboards, business insights, forecasts, recommendations, and decision intelligence.

Instead of acting as a traditional dashboard or spreadsheet viewer, Nexora AI automatically analyzes uploaded business data and generates a unique executive analytics experience for every dataset.

---

### Smart Data Upload

* CSV Support
* XLSX Support
* XLS Support
* TSV Support
* Data Quality Validation
* Error Detection

* Dataset-specific dashboard generation
* Power BI-inspired layouts
* Dynamic KPI cards

* Executive analytics views

* Executive Summary
* Trend Analysis
* Opportunity Detection
* Risk Identification
